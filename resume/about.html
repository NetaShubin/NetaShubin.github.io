About


Neta Shubin is a freshman at the University of Southern California. She has taken programming courses as part of her high school's academic curriculum, as well as participated in additional programs offered by both MIT and Harvard.

She has worked pro bono to create websites for local businesses, increasing their online presence and boosting their clientele. She has also organized and hosted an annual global hackathon, NeoHacks, in the summers of 2021 and 2022, encouraging high school students all over the world to continue exploring this vast field in computer science.
