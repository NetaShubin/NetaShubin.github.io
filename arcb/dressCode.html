All students should attend each lesson, be on time and properly dressed. Any student who is not dressed properly may not be permitted to join the class. Maintaining proper uniform sets a tone of professionalism and respect for the ballet school and its teachers and training, and is thus required for ALL students enrolled in ARCB. The ballet uniform for girls and young women consists of a leotard (color assigned by level), Ballet Pink Convertible Tights, and Pink Ballet shoes (slippers). Students are expected to maintain proper uniform at all times, including during production rehearsals, and private classes.

Girls:

Choose a level:

Select a level and learn the specified dresscode!

For the ballet-gymnastic practice: small blanket, leotard, shirts or sport pants, socks, and ballet slippers.

Boys:

White t-shirt or leotard, black fitted athletic shorts (i.e., bike shorts) or black tights, white socks, and white canvas ballet slippers. For the character practice: black jazz shoes. For the ballet-gymnastic practice: small blanket, white t-shirt or leotard, shorts or sports pants, socks, and white ballet slippers.


Adults:

No special dress requirements for the adult’s class students. Students may wear any comfortable clothing (leotard and tights, other exercise attire, etc.) that does not restrict movement and ballet shoes. Ballet slippers are still required.

Jazz/Lyrical/Modern/Contemporary:

Dancers may wear shorts or yoga pants and a tank top or t-shirt. Preferred to have jazz shoes or modern sandals. Canvas ballet slippers are OK.

Additional Rules:

  • All female students should wear ballet skirts for the character and pointe practice.
  • Every student must have canvas ballet slippers only! Light pink for girls and white for boys. Leather or semi-leather slippers and long sleeve leotards are not allowed! Pointe shoes must be either “Russian Pointe” or “Grishko” brands.
  • For ballet classes, rehearsals, and ARCB’s performances all-female dancers should use ballet pink convertible tights by «Capezio» only!
  • Girls’ hair must be pulled back and placed in a ballet bun at the crown of the head for all dance classes and rehearsals. The bun must comprise a hair net and hairpins or bobby pins, all of which should match the color of the student’s hair as closely as possible. Bangs must be smoothed back against the head using clips, pins, gel, or hairspray. Additionally, large hair adornments may not be worn; however, items such as small elastic headbands, small barrettes, ribbons, etc., may be worn at the instructor’s or Artistic Director’s discretion.
  • Students are asked to refrain from wearing jewelry during class, with the exception of small stud earrings.
  • No street shoes or hard-soled shoes are allowed on the dance floor.
