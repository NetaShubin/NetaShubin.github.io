About ARCB

Academy of Russian Classical Ballet is a pre-professional ballet school in the Seattle area committed to providing the best classical ballet training to students of all ages. Founded in 2009, Academy of Russian Classical Ballet (ARCB) has followed the Vaganova curriculum creating an extremely high level of ballet education through year-round ballet lessons, summer intensive programs, annual master classes with various internationally renowned guest teachers as well as countless performance opportunities.

Students from ARCB have been accepted into top international schools such as The Bolshoi Ballet Academy In Moscow, Russia, and The Kirov Academy of Ballet in Washington, D.C. Additionally, many of our students have placed at a number of Seattle YAGP regional competitions throughout the years and have received invitations to perform in the New York City Finals as soloists, Pas de Deuxâ€™s, and ensemble group piece members.

ARCB faculty follows the Vaganova curriculum that provides students with a structured, scientific dance methodology as well as encompasses a holistic pedagogical approach. We offer the most consecutive and competent technique system which is recognized as the best in the world. Our curriculum provides a solid foundation from which our students can achieve their greatest artistic potential.

Our beginning classes, for students ages three through five, provide developmentally appropriate dance education for our young dancers. For students ages six and older, we offer formal instruction in the Vaganova Method as well as additional contemporary and modern lessons. These levels are for the students who would like to improve beyond the fundamentals of ballet. Students will work on improving their flexibility and begin to learn the more complicated steps and patterns of classical ballet.

We are committed to providing our students with the highest quality of education, preserving the traditions of Vaganova through a systematic approach that is based on the culture and curriculum of the Vaganova Method. At our Academy, we strive to provide a safe and nurturing environment where our students can learn and grow as ballet students and people.
